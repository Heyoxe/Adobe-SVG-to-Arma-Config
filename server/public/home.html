Adobe XD to Arma 3

Create your GUIs in Adobe XD, export them as SVGs, use them in Arma 3

    Limitations
    • Adobe XD to Arma 3 (XD2A3 hereafter) will not accept Artboards format other than 1920x1080
    • XD2A3 will not export elements other than Rectangles or Groups
    • XD2A3 may or may not export your GUI if you don't name your elements
    • XD2A3 will not export your GUI if you have borders on any of your element
    • How do I know if my SVG is not compatible? If you upload it and nothing happens, it's not. If you think that you've followed all the steps correctly, please open an Issue on GitHub
